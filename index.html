Research Data Management

We provide comprehensive guidance on the management of research data across the entire data life cycle, from data generation and processing to analysis, documentation of data and metadata, data sharing, and long-term preservation. This includes expert support in the development of standard data management plans to ensure compliance from the start. Our support aims to ensures that your data remains well-organized, compliant with standards, and impactful at every stage. We support this by, for example, creating documentation templates, defining consistent folder structures, selecting suitable metadata standards, establishing data access controls, and implementing other tailored measures.

AI Enabled data management workflows and FAIR Data

We provide expert guidance in the development and implementation of AI enabled data management workflows designed to optimize critical phases of the data life cycle. Our services cover areas such as data documentation, automated metadata extraction, intelligent access control, GDPR compliance, and dynamic drafting of Data Management Plans (DMPs). We also ensure that all practices align with evolving AI regulations, including the EU AI Act. In addition, we provide guidance on implementing AI driven FAIR data practices to ensure datasets consistently meet the principles of being Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. We advise on embedding FAIR principles into data workflows, enhancing the efficiency of data discovery and reusability while ensuring alignment with established research data management frameworks. In addition, we carry out FAIR assessments to evaluate and strengthen the quality and compliance of data.

Open Science

We offer universities and research institutes expert consultancy to navigate European Commission’s Open Science requirements. Our services include guidance on Horizon Europe policy alignment, FAIR-compliant data management practices, data repository and data sharing license selection, Data Management Plans and tailored training. We also support the implementation of practices that embed Open Science into daily workflows, enabling institutions to meet EU mandates while fostering transparency, collaboration, and sustainable impact.
